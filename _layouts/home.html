The Suśruta Project

A project on the ancient medical literature of India

Critical study of the earliest manuscripts of the Compendium of Suśruta — a Sanskrit medical encyclopaedia composed two millennia ago — establishing the Nepalese version of the text through manuscript collation, digital edition, and translation.

Project 1

Textual and Cultural History of Medicine in South Asia based on Newly-Discovered Manuscript Evidence

2020–2024

Concluded April 2024. Publications include the first monograph, On the Plastic Surgery of the Ears and Nose: The Nepalese Version of the Suśrutasaṃhitā (2023), available in print and as a free PDF (DOI). The most recent article: Brooks, "On Leeches in a Medical Treatise for Humans" (2026). Work on further publications continues.
